About Biz4U

BIZ4U PTE LTD is a Singapore based consulting and trading company in Asia Pacific.

Given the rapid development of the international pandemic and to contribute to our fight against the COVID-19, the company has taken urgent action and decided to cooperate with local well-known medical companies to provide medical supplies such as disposable masks, protective suit, rubber gloves, goggles, thermometer and other protective masks.

We certify that all medical supplies we offer have obtained relevant certifications and are of reliable quality.

We offer prompt and secure deliveries
